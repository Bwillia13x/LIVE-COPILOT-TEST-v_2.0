Strategic Analysis Summary
NVIDIA's competitive position is anchored by three core Sustained Competitive Advantages: the CUDA ecosystem, proprietary GPU architecture, and R&D innovation capabilities. These resources are valuable, rare, difficult to imitate, and well-organized, creating deep moats around the business.

The company maintains Temporary Competitive Advantages in AI chip performance and market positioning, though these are more vulnerable to competitive pressure over time. Critical vulnerabilities exist in manufacturing capabilities, where reliance on TSMC creates strategic dependency.
Key Strategic Insight
NVIDIA's dominance stems from ecosystem lock-in effects rather than hardware superiority alone. The CUDA platform creates switching costs that sustain competitive advantage even as chip performance gaps may narrow over time.
